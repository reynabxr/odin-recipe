Lasagna

Description

A great lasagna only needs two things: a thick, rich, super-meaty sauce (and lots of it!) and a ricotta filling where only the finest cheeses are welcomed. For the sauce, I love a 50-50 combo of Italian sausage and lean ground beef.

Ingredients

  • Onion, garlic, carrot and celery - for the flavour base, a soffrito;
  • Beef
  • Canned tomato
  • Red Wine

Steps

  1. Smear a bit of meat sauce on the base first - stops the lasagna sheets from sliding around;
  2. top with meat sauce, bit of white sauce
  3. cover with lasagna sheets, pour over remaining white sauce then sprinkle with cheese.
